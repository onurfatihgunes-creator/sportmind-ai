SportMind AI

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

SportMind AI ("the app") is a sports analysis app that shows AI-generated, statistics-based predictions for football and basketball matches. This policy explains what data the app handles and, just as importantly, what it doesn't.

We don't collect personal data

SportMind AI has no user accounts, no sign-up, and no login. We don't collect your name, email address, location, or any other personal information, and we don't use advertising or analytics tracking of any kind.

What's stored, and where

A small set of preferences is stored locally on your own device only, and is never sent to us or any third party:

  • Your chosen display name, if you set one (defaults to "Player")
  • Your selected app language
  • Matches you've bookmarked to your personal watchlist

Uninstalling the app deletes this data, since it never leaves your device.

Match and prediction data

To show you matches and AI predictions, the app reads (read-only) data from our backend, which in turn sources fixtures and results from football-data.org and balldontlie.io. This is sports data only — no personal information about you is sent as part of these requests.

Children's privacy

SportMind AI is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect data from anyone, regardless of age.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.
