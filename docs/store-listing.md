## Full description (max ~4000 chars, both stores)

SportMind AI is an AI-powered sports intelligence app that analyses football and
basketball matches using real statistical data — recent form, expected goals/points,
and head-to-head trends — and explains its reasoning in plain language.

This is **not** a betting app. SportMind AI doesn't accept wagers, doesn't show odds,
and doesn't facilitate gambling in any way. Every analysis is presented as a
statistical likelihood, not a prediction or guarantee.

**What you get:**
- Win-probability estimates for upcoming football (Premier League, La Liga, Serie A,
  Bundesliga, Ligue 1, Champions League) and NBA basketball matches
- A clear "why" behind every analysis — head-to-head factor comparisons like recent
  form, expected goals/points, and home advantage
- Team profiles with recent form and upcoming fixtures
- A personal watchlist to save matches you care about
- Compare two teams side by side
- Available in English, Turkish, German, and Arabic (with full right-to-left support)

**Why trust the numbers?**
SportMind AI is transparent about its limitations. Every analysis carries a clear
disclaimer: outcomes remain unpredictable, and this is statistical analysis, not
advice. You can always see exactly which factors drove a given estimate.

SportMind AI is currently free to use.
